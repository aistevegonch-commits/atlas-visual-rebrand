Upcoming Decisions You Cannot Avoid

Month 3
Can I afford to keep going?
Proceed if: Revenue >$3K OR at least 2 proposals sent with active pipeline.
Quit/pivot if: Zero revenue AND zero proposals. Something is fundamentally wrong — niche, pricing, or outreach volume.

Month 6
Is this actually working?
Proceed if: 2+ clients closed, $5K+ revenue, 1+ repeat conversation in motion.
Quit/pivot if: Revenue <$8K with zero repeats. The market is telling you something. Listen.
NEED 2+ CLIENTS, $5K+
Month 12
Hire or stay solo?
Hire if: Trailing 6-month avg >$12K/mo, cash reserve >$25K, consistent >3 projects/mo.
Stay solo if: Avg $8K–12K/mo. Keep using contract editors. Do not add payroll until revenue is locked in.
NEED $12K+/MO TRAILING
Month 18
Scale or stay small?
Scale if: Local revenue >$250K, net margin >25%, systems documented.
Stay small if: Revenue $150K–250K. There is no shame in a profitable solo practice. Ego kills businesses.
NEED $250K+ & MARGIN >25%
⚠ The Honest Truth About Quitting
Quitting is not failure. Pouring another 12 months and $20K into a model that the market has already rejected — that is failure. Set these criteria now, when you are calm. Do not move the goalposts when Month 6 arrives and you are stressed.
Red Flags

These Mean You Need to Act Immediately

If 2+ red flags are active, you are in crisis mode. Stop everything and fix the biggest one.

1
Cash below $3,000. You are one unexpected expense away from not making rent. Pause all non-essential spending. Do not buy a new lens.
2
No paid projects by Month 3. This will take longer than you think, but not this long. If you have zero revenue at 90 days, your outreach, pricing, or niche is broken.
3
Client concentration >60% in one client. One client should never have the power to put you out of business. If they leave, you are starting from zero. Diversify now.
4
Editor ghosting + deadline approaching. You have no backup editor. This will happen. Build a bench of 2–3 reliable editors before you need them.
5
Working >60 hrs/week but revenue <$3K/mo. You are not running a business. You are running a very expensive hobby. The math does not work at this pace.
6
No outreach activity for 7+ days. If you are not prospecting, you are hoping. Hope is not a strategy. The pipeline empties faster than you think.
⚠ Crisis Mode Protocol
If 2+ flags are active: (1) Stop all spending except survival essentials. (2) Spend 80% of your time on sales and outreach. (3) Drop your prices 20% temporarily if it closes work this week. (4) Do not sleep on it. Act today.
