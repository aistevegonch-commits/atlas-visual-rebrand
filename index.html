Team Structure

Solo to Scaled. No Bloat.

Hire for revenue, not overhead. Each role is added only when pipeline justifies the cost.

Month 1

Solo Operator

You film, edit, sell, and deliver. Capital-light. Every dollar earned is profit. Perfect for proving product-market fit before adding cost.

Month 3

+ Editor

First contractor: a vetted remote editor who handles first cuts and revisions. You focus on shooting and client relationships. Turnaround drops. Volume rises.

Month 6

+ Producer

A part-time producer manages scheduling, permits, and logistics. You direct the creative. The machine runs without your constant input.

Month 12

+ Capture Network

Trusted local videographers in key markets capture B-roll and secondary angles. You edit and deliver under the Atlas Visual brand. Geographic scale unlocked.
