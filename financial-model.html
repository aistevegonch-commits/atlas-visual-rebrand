06 / Decisions

The Decision Matrix — Month 12

A hard framework for the Year 1 review. No ambiguity. Revenue, repeat business, and growth trend determine the path forward.

Revenue < $60K, zero repeat clients, and declining or flat month-over-month
QUIT
Revenue $60K–$100K, 1–2 repeat clients, flat trend — you have product-market fit but no engine
PIVOT
Revenue > $100K, 3+ repeat clients, and a growing trend — the machine is working
DOUBLE DOWN

How to Read This

The Decision Matrix is designed to remove emotion from the Year 1 review. Most solo founders either quit too early or scale too late. This framework forces you to look at three objective signals:

  • Revenue — is the business generating enough to justify the owner's time?
  • Repeat clients — does the product create loyalty, or is every sale a new hunt?
  • Trend — is momentum building, stalling, or reversing?

If you are in the PIVOT zone, the move is not to work harder — it is to raise prices, narrow the niche, or change the offer. If you are in the DOUBLE DOWN zone, the move is to hire, market harder, and expand capacity.
