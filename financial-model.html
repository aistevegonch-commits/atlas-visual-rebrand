07 / Seasonality

Seasonality Adjustment

Your "average" monthly target means nothing. Plan for Q1 to suck. 60-70% of revenue lands in Q2-Q3.

Quarter Revenue Multiplier Months What to Expect Cash Reserve Target
Q1 0.6x Jan – Mar Slow. Clients are spending down budgets. New projects are rare. End with $4,000+
Q2 1.3x Apr – Jun Peak season. Budgets refresh. Event season starts. Book everything you can. End with $8,000+
Q3 1.2x Jul – Sep Strong. Summer events, fall campaigns starting. Don't slow down. End with $6,000+
Q4 0.9x Oct – Dec Mixed. Holiday slowdown but year-end budget dumps possible. End with $5,000+
Survival math: If your annual target is $100K, don't plan for $8,333/mo. Plan for $5,000 in Q1 and $10,800 in Q2. The quarters where you make money have to carry the quarters where you don't. Most businesses die in Q1 because they spent Q2's money in December.

This model uses optimistic assumptions about your efficiency. Reality will be 20-30% worse.
